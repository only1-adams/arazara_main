We help Community Enterprises embrace financial freedom

ARAZARA is dedicated to MSMEs, we are an Africa-oriented communities enterprising driven company focused on SME-capacity building and Technology products.  ARAZARA has been in the business of Training (design and delivery), Counseling workshops, Conferences and other Knowledge events which are MSME related and has a number of activities that focus on Retraining, Sensitizing and Mobilizing of clusters and rendering devices that promote and sustain the defined objectives of institutions wishing to penetrate the SME sector with Programs, Projects, Ideas, Goods and Services.  We help Community Enterprises embrace financial freedom through Skill Acquisition, Entrepreneurship and Self-reliance by Identifying, Categorizing, Organizing and Profiling them into clusters, training them in the business aspect of their skill as value providers in their Production/Distribution chain or as Consumers of services that support them, providing them with access to Market, access to Information and access to Funds by our collaborations with Multilateral bodies that assist our activities through Advocacy and Capacity Building Initiatives. The only way we know how to do it at Arazara. We have Curriculum Development Services for Schools, Legislatives, Businesses, Non-profit, Community Enterprises and Rural Settlement Groups looking to add Knowledge and Training programmes in existing sectors of the economy, helping our clients meet their objectives and achieve return on investment. It engages participants to  end up noticeably confident and fabricate some level of fearlessness in them. In fact, it is a life changing experience.

Our Purpose and Vision

Our brand’s PURPOSE is fueled by our original commitment to Community Enterprises giving them hope, aspirations and added value. We are equable to make consistent standard in Institutional capacity Building and excellence in Training and Development programmes across Africa, in consonance with our VISION, To remain a reputable, resourceful, and responsive organization, having support solutions rooted in documentaries and research that unlock and enhance the productive and evolving energies of individuals/SME entrepreneurs and anchor the efforts of governments.

Our Mission Statement

At Arazara, our MISSION is to look at these sectoral clusters wherever they may be, realize their full potential, find out their challenges and see how we can assist them to function better and make them produce competitively by stimulating government to improve on social investments which will reduce the burden of poverty while increasing economic growth. Everything we do reflects this mission and the three codes of belief that we hold;

• Keep them armed with the latest tools and technology available
